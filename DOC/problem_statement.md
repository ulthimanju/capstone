# Problem Statement

Students struggle with fragmented learning — notes in Google Docs, practice problems on LeetCode, courses on separate platforms, and no system connecting any of it. There is no single place to upload study material, get AI-generated quizzes and flashcards from it, track coding practice progress, and stay motivated through structured goals.

**Capestone** solves this by giving students one unified platform where their documents become an intelligent, closed knowledge base — generating quizzes, flashcards, and summaries automatically — while also tracking coding practice progress and rewarding consistent learning through XP, badges, and skill trees.
